About the Game

Tanuki Sunset is a relaxing longboarding arcade game where you play as a stylish raccoon skating downhill through neon-lit roads. Your goal is to drift smoothly, avoid obstacles, collect points, and enjoy the chill synthwave atmosphere while cruising toward the sunset. The game focuses on smooth controls, stylish tricks, and endless downhill fun.

Game Information

Game NameTanuki Sunset
GenreArcade / Skateboarding
PlatformBrowser & PC
Game ModeSingle Player
ObjectiveDrift, score points, and avoid crashes

How to Play

  1. Click the Play Now button to start the game.
  2. Use arrow keys or A/D keys to steer.
  3. Hold drift while turning to earn combo points.
  4. Avoid hitting cars, walls, and obstacles.
  5. Collect items and maintain smooth rides to get high scores.
